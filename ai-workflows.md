# AI Workflows

This document maps how AI systems interact inside the CareQuest ecosystem.

The goal is not simply to use AI for conversation.

The goal is to build AI-native workforce training infrastructure.

---

# Core AI Workflow Layers

1. Learner Understanding
2. Adaptive Learning
3. Simulation Evaluation
4. Competency Scoring
5. AI Supervision
6. Workforce Readiness Prediction

---

# 1. Learner Understanding Workflow

## Goal

Understand:
- learner goals
- learning speed
- competency gaps
- emotional confidence
- progression style

## Inputs

- onboarding assessment
- completed missions
- simulation responses
- retention behavior
- XP progression

## Outputs

- personalized mission path
- weak competency areas
- recommended simulations
- progression difficulty

---

# 2. Adaptive Learning Workflow

## Goal

Personalize learning progression.

## AI Functions

- mission recommendation
- difficulty adjustment
- spaced repetition
- remediation suggestions
- streak recovery prompts

## Example

If a learner struggles repeatedly with:
- reinforcement
- transition planning

the system automatically:
- lowers complexity
- adds practice simulations
- unlocks remediation missions

---

# 3. Simulation Evaluation Workflow

## Goal

Evaluate learner responses in realistic behavioral scenarios.

## AI Evaluates

- empathy
- safety
- observation
- reinforcement
- communication
- practical next steps

## Outputs

- competency score
- coaching summary
- improved response example
- recommended next mission

---

# 4. Competency Scoring Workflow

## Goal

Generate dynamic competency profiles.

## Inputs

- simulation responses
- mission performance
- retry frequency
- supervision feedback
- assessment scores

## Outputs

- competency graph
- readiness score
- growth areas
- strengths
- confidence trends

---

# 5. AI Supervision Workflow

## Goal

Reduce supervision bottlenecks.

## AI Supervisor Tasks

- review learner responses
- summarize progress
- identify patterns
- recommend intervention focus
- generate supervision notes

## Long-Term Vision

AI-assisted supervision infrastructure for care workforce scaling.

---

# 6. Workforce Readiness Prediction

## Goal

Estimate learner readiness for certification and employment.

## Inputs

- competency history
- simulation consistency
- mission completion
- remediation history
- behavioral strengths

## Outputs

- readiness score
- certification confidence
- role recommendations
- placement readiness

---

# Long-Term Infrastructure Vision

Over time, CareQuest could evolve into:

- competency intelligence layer
- care workforce graph
- simulation infrastructure
- AI supervision layer
- global workforce readiness engine

for neurodivergence care.
